Introducing Common Checklist

Common Checklist is an attempt to simplify the college application process for JPIS students. Basically serving as an intermediary to the Common Application, we provide a portal for students and their counsellors to upload important college-related documents such as essays, standaradised testing scores, activity lists, etc.

With Common Checklist, Kepler Tech hopes to aid you in your college application process. Get started today!
